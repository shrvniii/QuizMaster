BHARAT VIKAS PARISHAD

Holistic Development of Bharat Through Seva and Sanskar

Established in **1963**, Bharat Vikas Parishad (BVP) is a service-cum-sanskar oriented, non-political, socio-cultural voluntary organization. With over **2,000 branches** and more than **1 Lakh members** nationwide, it operates through **10 Regions** and **80 Prants** to serve society and nurture values.

The Five Sutras (Principles)

SAMPARK Contact / Connection
SAHYOG Cooperation / Collaboration
SANSKAR Moral & Cultural Values
SERVICE Social & Civic Duty
SAMARPAN Commitment / Dedication

About "Bharat Ko Jano" Quiz

**"Bharat Ko Jano"** is a prominent nationwide quiz and cultural awareness program organized by the Bharat Vikas Parishad. It is designed to inspire and deepen citizens' and students' appreciation of India's rich heritage, history, geographical vastness, scientific accomplishments, and democratic achievements.

Age Categories

Junior Group

Students of Classes 6 to 8

Senior Group

Students of Classes 9 to 12

Core Quiz Syllabus

  • Indian Heritage & Culture: Ancient scriptures, traditions, religions, fine arts, architecture, and languages.
  • History & Geography: Sacred landscapes, historical struggles, freedom movement, and natural topography.
  • Constitutional & Democratic Values: Parliamentary systems, fundamental rights, duties, and statecraft.
  • Current Affairs & Achievements: India's recent developments in science, defense, infrastructure, space research, and global leadership.
