Scanning Guidelines

  • Ensure the **four black square registration marks** in the corners of the sheet are completely visible and not covered by fingers, shadows, or folds.
  • Take the photo directly from above to minimize perspective distortion, and ensure the page is flat.
  • Capture in **good lighting** to avoid heavy shadows across the bubbles. Clear natural light or bright indoor lighting works best.
  • Ensure the student has filled the bubbles dark and completely. Light markings may not be detected.
